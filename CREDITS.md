## Scientific References

- Galli & Palla (1998), A&A 335, 403: early-universe chemistry network.
- Güsten et al. (2019), Nature 568, 357: astrophysical detection of HeH+ in
  NGC 7027 with SOFIA/upGREAT.
- Pachucki (2012), Phys. Rev. A 85, 042511: accurate Born-Oppenheimer
  potential and equilibrium geometry.
- Pavanello et al. (2005), J. Chem. Phys. 123, 104306: non-Born-Oppenheimer
  vibrational calculations and dipole reference used in later line-list work.
- Bishop & Cheung (1979), J. Mol. Spectrosc. 75, 462: spectroscopic constants.
- Stanke et al. (2006), Phys. Rev. A 73, 012503: non-Born-Oppenheimer
  rovibrational/binding data.
